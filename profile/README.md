# Post GPT Era Education Framework!

## Curriculum

### **The Process**
Somewhat equivalent of admission process. 

It'll be completely merit based through three gamified platforms.
There won't be any annual admission tests but rather it'll be throughout the year. 
The students who'll top on the leaderboard in those platforms will meet the eligibility criteria.

> Short description of those three platform:
### Ganit (Computation)
This platform is dedicated to test the mental abilities (IQ) of the child.

Areas of focus:
- Practical Mathematics
- Pattern Recognition
- General Intelligence (Logic, Reasoning & Planning)
- Basic Programming (Harvard CS50 by David J Malan)

### Shiksha (Humanity)
This platform is dedicated to test the linguistic ability (EQ) of the child.

Areas of focus:
- Textual Comprehension (Reading)
- Typing (Writing & Effective Articulation)
- Audio-visual Comprehension (Speech & Videos)
- Language
    - Prakrit (Mother Tongue/Native/vernacular language.)
    - Sanskrit ( Link language for precise expression, learning, discussions & debate )
    - English ( Current global standard for language)
    - Python ( Easy to learn, high level programming language for Algorithm development )
    - Javascript ( Easy to learn, high level programming language for Interface development )

### Veda (General Knowledge)
This platform is dedicated to test the retention & analytical ability of the child.

Areas of focus:
- Critical Thinking.
- Health & Fitness.
- Big bang & basic knowledge of solar system, earth.
- Basic Physics & Chemistry
- Evolution
- Human history
- Mythology & religions.
- Continents, countries & Belief systems
- Capitalism
- Human values & Differnt ways of living
- Discovary & Inventions
- First Principle Thinking
- Zeroth Principle Thinking
- Basic Knowledge of
    - Dharmashastra
    - Arthashastra
    - Karmashastra
    - Sevashastra

#### Guidelines for using this Platforms.

There will be multiple games to play.
Play any games to score points & rank in leaderboard.
Upon scoring in leaderboard, you'll win rings as achievements.

You can also join tournaments to compete with other students & win real prizes.

### **The Programs**
Somewhat equivalent of classes.

It'll be based on skills and ability of the student.
It is structured in such a way, anyone can integrate & upskill themselves along the way.

> Short description of the programs:
### Literacy
The first program will be completely online & it'll majorly focus on getting digital/future literacy.
It is structured in such a way that students accustomed to different ways can integrate to maverick way of doing things & experiment for themselves wheather it is suited for them or not without any commitments.

Areas of focus:
- Health Literacy (Sleep, Diet, Workout)
- Financial Literacy. ( Personal finance, How money works)
- Cultural Literacy. (Develop resistance to Indoctrination/Manipulation, Know your culture better)
- Programming Literacy. ( Content | Design | Interface | Algorithm Development)
- Self Authoring & Future (Direction) Planning.
- How to Learn(Best learning Methods)
- Verbal & Non-verbal Communication
